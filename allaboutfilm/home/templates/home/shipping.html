Shipping Policy

This Shipping Policy outlines how we handle order delivery. By placing an order, you acknowledge and agree to the shipping terms presented at checkout.

Any shipping estimates shown on product pages are provided for reference only. The final shipping options, costs, and delivery details for your order will be displayed during checkout.

Shipping methods, pricing, and delivery estimates may change from time to time. The shipping terms shown at checkout will apply to your order.

Price

Shipping prices are flat rates which vary based on your selected shipping method, destination, and package size.

Shipping Countries

We ship to most regions, including the Americas, Europe, Asia, and Australia/Oceania. If no shipping options are available at checkout, please contact us for a custom quote at info@aaf.com.

Please note, we do not deliver to PO boxes, so be sure to provide a physical address during checkout.

Shipping & Handling Times

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1–4 business days. Processing times may be extended during peak periods such as sales or holidays.

Safety

Orders are safely and securely packed, include shipping insurance, and come with a tracking number.

Customs & Duties

All About Film ships all non-EU orders using Delivered Duty Paid (DDP). This means all applicable taxes, duties, and customs fees are calculated and paid at checkout, so your order arrives without additional charges on delivery. For any questions about VAT & customs, reach us at info@aaf.com.
