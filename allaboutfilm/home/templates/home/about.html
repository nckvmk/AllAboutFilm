Meet The Team

Nick Vamvakas
(Owner/IT Technician/Luthier)

Since early high school I have always been fascinated by blues and classic rock music. That was also right around the time I picked up playing the guitar. Although I started as a self-taught acoustic player, I've grown to love electric guitars and amps more than anything in this world. Besides the self-expressive and artistic aspect of being a musician, I also love using vintage music gear. I am telling you these old timers really got some mojo in them that modern stuff doesn't quite have. Besides only playing guitars, I also find joy in fixing them up and customizing them. All that, coupled with the fact that trading vintage music gear is kind of a niche market in Greece, are what made me to dive into this line of business. Furthermore, I am majoring in IT, so I also handle and maintain all the digital infrastructure of my business.

George Vamvakas
(Sales Coordinator & Logistics)

I cannot say that I was particularly interested in music until my brother started playing. He would share with me everything that he had picked up each time, and he would motivate me to give it a go as well. Although I am not as passionate about music and guitars as he is, I like playing from time to time. I am studying economics so, when he offered me a spot on his store as a sales coordinator, I knew it would be a nice addition to my resume.

Kostantinos Dimitrakopoulos
(Customer Support)

Me and Nick have been friends since forever. He would always find a way to sneak-in his acoustic during school events, and together with other friends we would have a blast. Seeing him become the guitar connoisseur he is now, once made me jokingly suggest to open up a guitar shop. And yet here we are... I have a major in Psychology, and I have worked front-desk at my father's hardware store for some years. So, when he offered me a customer support role in his company, I grinned and he said "Welcome on board, you are starting this Monday".

Why You Can Trust Us

For collectors and musicians alike, confidence is essential when acquiring vintage instruments. Our reputation is built on expertise, transparency, and a genuine passion for historically significant guitars and amplifiers.

Every piece in our collection undergoes a thorough evaluation and is documented with detailed descriptions, high-resolution photography, and accurate condition reporting. Whenever possible, we provide information regarding originality, modifications, provenance, and service history, ensuring that customers can make informed purchasing decisions.

We conduct transactions both through our showroom and our Reverb storefront, offering secure and established purchasing channels backed by clear communication and professional customer service. Whether acquiring a player's-grade instrument or a rare collector's piece, customers can expect the same level of care, integrity, and attention to detail throughout the process.

Our goal is not simply to facilitate transactions, but to build lasting relationships with musicians, collectors, and enthusiasts who share our appreciation for exceptional vintage gear.
